Privacy Terms

Privacy Policy

Updated May 2026

Rahul Pahuja ("we," "us," or "our") operates the DeDup mobile application. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app.

🛡️ Local Processing Guarantee: All file scanning, hashing, and duplicate detection happen entirely on your device. No file names, contents, or metadata from your storage are ever transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: If you log in via Google or Facebook, we collect your name, email, and profile picture to personalize your experience.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique identifiers via Firebase Analytics to improve app performance.
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics collects logs when the app crashes to help us fix bugs.

2. Permissions & Data Usage

  • All Files Access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Allows scanning your entire storage to identify duplicate documents, zip files, and other files consuming space. File hashes are computed locally only — we do not read the content of your personal documents for any purpose other than deduplication.
  • Media Permissions (Images, Video, Audio): Allows scanning your gallery and music library for duplicate photos, videos, and songs. Used to generate thumbnails for preview and perform comparisons to find exact duplicates.
  • Notifications: To alert you when storage is running low or a scan is complete. No marketing notifications are sent without your explicit consent.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Google Play Services / Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Auth)
  • Facebook Login SDK

4. Facebook Data Deletion

To delete data obtained via Facebook Login:

  1. Remove the app from your Facebook Settings under Apps and Websites.
  2. Contact us at the email below to request manual deletion of your account data.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & DPDP)

Depending on your location (EU under GDPR or India under DPDP Act), you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Data Portability in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent for analytics at any time via app settings
  • Nominate an individual to exercise your rights (DPDP specific)

6. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of a Contract: To provide the core cleaning features you requested.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve app performance via crash reports and analytics.
  • Consent: When you voluntarily log in via social media or opt-in to certain features.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SQLCipher) to protect your local scan history and settings stored on your device.
